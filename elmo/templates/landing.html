ELMO

An EVE Online app for moon mining

Crowdsourced Moon Analysis

Harness the collective scanning power of your entire alliance. Using fine-grained permissions you can allow your alliance members to submit results while not necessarily being able to view the scans of others.

Coordinate Mining Activities

Keep track of when your next mining fleet will have to be using the moon mining timerboard. Gain insight in the minerals and products you will gain from mining certain moons.

It's 100% Open Source

Since ELMO is under the MIT license you are free to change the source code however you want to fit your needs. You can also host your own instance of ELMO to avoid your precious scanning results from falling in the wrong hands!
